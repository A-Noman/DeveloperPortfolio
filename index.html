My Story

Hello! My name is Asad and I’m currently a senior studing Software Engineering at The University of North Carolina Charlotte (UNCC).

Not sure if I'll ever be able to call myself a "software engineer" but I do call myself a problem solver, a deep thinker, and a master of rubber duck debugging.

My coding journey started when I volunteered at a coding bootcamp with half of a biology degree and absolutely zero knowledge of programming. The summer after the bootcamp I changed majors, transferred universities, and began actually loving what I was doing. The education is a means to an end for me and that end is creating accessible, inclusive, and helpful products that make a difference.

Some technologies I'm currently working with include:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Node.js
  • Express
  • MongoDB
